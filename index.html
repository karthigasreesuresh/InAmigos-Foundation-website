Milestones & History

The Journey of InAmigos

Tracing our evolution from a localized grassroots pandemic support initiative in Chhattisgarh to a structured national movement covering environmental, animal, educational, and vocational empowerment tracks.

September 23, 2020

Inception & Emergency Relief

InAmigos Foundation was established by Mr. Govind Shukla in Sipat, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. The foundation was licensed as a Section 8 NGO and instantly launched Project Seva, distributing critical sanitation kits and meals during pandemic lockdowns.

Mid 2021 Development

Expanding to Education & Welfare

Launched Project Bachpanshala to address learning gaps in rural areas through digital literacy classes. Simultaneously launched Project Jeev, building local feeding points for stray animals during peak summers.

Late 2022 Accreditations

Securing Tax Exemption Certifications

Secured official 12A and 80G tax exemptions, cementing donor trust. Formalized Project Udaan, establishing sewing and vocational training clusters for self-help groups (SHGs).

2023 Scaled Operations

National Skills Platform Launch

Obtained NITI Aayog registration and ISO 9001:2015 certification. Scaled Project Vikas, providing structured domain-level internships for youth to enhance post-college employment opportunities.

2024 - Present Phase

CSR Compliance & Nationwide Scope

Earned CSR-1 certification. Expanded Project Prakriti, planting over 20,000+ trees. The organization's projects have now trained over 30,000+ interns and served 50,000+ meals across India.

Our Initiatives

Critical Social Operations

Food Relief
Initiative 01

Project Seva

Addressing nutritional crises and clothing shortages by serving underprivileged and low-income families across urban shelters and rural clusters.

  • Weekly food drives and clothing packets
  • Shelter distributions across 15+ cities
  • Direct nutritional assistance programs
Literacy
Initiative 02

Project Bachpanshala

Bridging the digital gap by equipping children with core skills: digital applications, computer logic, communication, and primary mentorship.

  • Foundational computer operations classes
  • Critical basic life skills and values training
  • Free books and elementary study support
Animal Welfare
Initiative 03

Project Jeev

Protecting street populations by conducting daily feeding drives, rabies vaccinations, summer water bowl installations, and medical rescues.

  • Daily feeding for 50+ stray animals
  • Clean water bowl placement in Bilaspur
  • Direct first-aid and medical rescue aid
Women Self-Help
Initiative 04

Project Udaan

Promoting self-reliance by training rural women in vocational skills like tailoring, and conducting menstrual hygiene and financial literacy seminars.

  • Support for rural self-help groups (SHGs)
  • Tailoring equipment and vocational support
  • Menstrual hygiene awareness campaigns
Environment
Initiative 05

Project Prakriti

Improving green cover and combating climate hazards by planting 20,000+ saplings and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

  • Over 20,000 saplings planted across states
  • Community green awareness workshops
  • Support for eco-friendly farming practices
Employment
Initiative 06

Project Vikas

Providing structured remote and hybrid internship pathways across key digital domains, training over 30,000+ interns.

  • Training in content, design, data, and social work
  • Upskilling program for 30,000+ certified interns
  • Career development and mentorship tracks

Our Purpose

Why Our Work Matters

Hunger Alleviation

Addressing nutritional deficiencies by targeting the immediate food security needs of homeless children and low-income families.

Educational Equity

Removing structural barriers to learning by giving underprivileged kids access to school books, digital devices, and basic mentoring.

Women Independence

Enabling equal economic participation by fostering leadership capacities and teaching high-demand skills to homemakers.

Animal Compassion

Cultivating compassion for all living beings, running veterinary drives, and keeping stray populations healthy and feed-supported.

Climatic Forestry

Mitigating climate hazards by promoting community forestry, raising environmental consciousness, and limiting plastic trash.

Employability Growth

Eliminating job hurdles for young graduates through mentorship and practical business experience in remote-work models.

Civic Welfare

Ensuring strong social safety nets through active volunteer teams ready to respond during disasters and public health drives.
